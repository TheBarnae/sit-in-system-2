About the University

The University of Cebu (UC) is one of the leading educational institutions in Cebu City, Philippines. Founded in 1964, UC is committed to providing quality education and producing globally competitive graduates. The university offers a wide range of programs across multiple disciplines and continues to uphold its mission of academic excellence and community service.

About the Department

The College of Computer Studies (CCS) is dedicated to producing skilled IT professionals equipped with the knowledge and competencies needed in the fast-evolving technology industry. CCS offers programs such as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS), and Associate in Computer Technology (ACT).

About this System

The CCS Sit-in Monitoring System is designed to track and manage student sit-in sessions within the CCS computer laboratories. It helps ensure proper monitoring of lab usage and maintains an organized record of student attendance.
